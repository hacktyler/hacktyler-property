Tyler home values, 2011

This map shows the assessed property tax value of privately owned residential properties in Tyler, Texas as of 2011.

Each color accounts for 20% of properties (approximately 5,264 out of 26,323 total).

For example, the 20% of properties valued at less than $33,999 are dark purple. White properties are those nearest the median value of $85,233.
